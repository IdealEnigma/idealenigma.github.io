Process

A taste of where you can go.

  • Consultation

    Let's get coffee!

    We want to hear about the problems you face as a small business, then build tools to solve them. You are an expert at your business and we want to hear about it. Consulations and quotes are always free!

  • Feedback

    After our intial meeting we will develop some potiential solutions within your budget, along with timelines and other options. Once you choose a solution, build phase begins!

  • Development and Review

    Continuous Improvement

    Development is broken into smaller phases, where you will be able to give feedback and direction to the development of your new product. Towards the end there will be a big review and trial phase, where we will adjust and perfect the product before the launch!

  • Launch!

    Enjoy your new product!

    Time to celebrate!

    Of Our
    Story!
